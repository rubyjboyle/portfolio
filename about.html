about

Hello, my name is Ruby Boyle. I am currently a graduate student at UC Irvine studying Human Computer Interaction and Design. I am also currently a UX Accessibility Engineer at an e-commerce site called shop.com.

I am passionate about the intersection of design and development and have had four years crafting these skills as a front-end developer. With my graduate program I am focusing on research applications of HCI within healthcare software, establishing heuristics, and data driven designs.

When I am not working or studying I enjoy getting outside, walking around DTLA, and discovering new art.
