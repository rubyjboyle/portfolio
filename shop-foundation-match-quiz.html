foundation match quiz

@shop.com

Project Description

Motives Cosmetics is one of the top beauty brands sold on shop.com. With the launch of their new product Motives Cosmetics Flawless Face Stick Foundation, there wasn't a way for the customer to know what their shade would be. This foundation match quiz was developed to have the user answer several questions to narrow down what their flawless match would be.

error

Problem

With the launch of the new product; Motives Cosmetics Flawless Face Stick Foundation there was not a way to virtually find your color match.

design_services

My Role

My roles for this project consisted of UX designer and front end developer. I designed the concepts for each quiz question page, how a user navigates through the quiz, and getting their final result. I also developed the HTML, SCSS, and JavaScript for the quiz as well.

flag

Goal

The goal was to provide the user an interactive way to get their perfect foundation shade match and easily add to cart and refer back to by email.

check_circle_outline

Outcome

The outcome of the foundation quiz was an overall increase in sales for the foundation.
