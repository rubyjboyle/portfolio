Course Description

Introduction to user-centered design and prototyping. Focused on practical methods for interaction design. Topics include the nature of design and the challenges to creating and evaluating good design, as well as specific skills for designing interactive systems.

Course Reflection

The three key things I learned this quarter are:

  • How to understand and apply design thinking, process and principles.
  • Create refined designs & prototypes through an iterative process.
  • How to effectively communicate and sell creative solutions in presentations
